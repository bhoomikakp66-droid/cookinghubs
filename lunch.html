RASAM

Rasam is a tangy, spicy, and savory South Indian soup or broth, a staple of the cuisine known for its digestive benefits.
It's made from a base of tamarind juice and tomatoes, flavored with a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and black pepper.

HEEREKAYI/RIDGE GOURD PAPU

Ridge gourd is one of the most commonly used vegetable in Indian cuisine.
Turai, Gilki, Beerakaya, Peerakangai are some of the regional names to this veggie.
Ridge gourd is used to make curry, added to dals & even made to a spicy chutney.

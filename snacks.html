Pancake

A pancake is a flat, often round cake made from a starch-based batter of flour, eggs, and milk, cooked on a hot griddle or frying pan.
It can be thick and fluffy, or thin and crispy, depending on the recipe, and is enjoyed worldwide with a variety of sweet or savory toppings like syrup, fruit, or vegetables.
The word "pancake" has origins in the Middle Ages, and the dish itself has a long history, with variations found in ancient cultures and across different countries today.

Momos

Momos are steamed or fried dumplings, popular in Tibetan, Nepalese, and Indian cuisine.
They typically consist of a dough filled with minced meat or vegetables,
seasoned with spices like garlic, ginger, and cilantro.
Momos are often served with a spicy dipping sauce called achar.
